# uomthesis: University of Manchester AMBS PhD Thesis Template and Compliance Tools

Provides a Quarto template (standard and journal formats), a project
scaffolder, a word counter, and a compliance validator for PhD theses
submitted to Alliance Manchester Business School (AMBS) in the Faculty
of Humanities at the University of Manchester. Encodes the requirements
of the University's Presentation of Theses Policy.
